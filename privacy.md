# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: January 27, 2026**

## Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Jeff ("I", "me", or "my") handles information in connection with the personal Whoop integration application ("the App").

## Information Collection

The App accesses the following data from your Whoop account:
- Sleep data (duration, stages, quality scores)
- Recovery scores and metrics
- Strain data and workout information
- Heart rate and HRV measurements

## How Information Is Used

This is a **personal use application**. Data accessed through the App is:
- Used solely for personal health tracking and automation
- Stored locally on private, secured systems
- Never sold, shared, or disclosed to third parties
- Never used for advertising or marketing purposes

## Data Storage

All data is stored on privately-owned infrastructure with appropriate security measures including:
- Encrypted storage
- Access controls
- Regular security updates

## Data Retention

Data is retained only as long as needed for personal use. You may request deletion of any stored data at any time.

## Third-Party Services

The App connects to the Whoop API to retrieve your data. Please refer to [Whoop's Privacy Policy](https://www.whoop.com/privacy/) for information on how Whoop handles your data.

## Children's Privacy

This App is not intended for use by children under 13 years of age.
